# Volunteering

Community, education, and humanitarian initiatives across Dubai and Birmingham.

---

## Digital Literacy Workshop — Speaker
**Millennium Campus Network (MCN) · Nov 2024**

Volunteered as a speaker at a Digital Literacy Workshop organised by Shailee Kampani, held for university support staff including janitors. Topics covered:

- Setting up Google Accounts
- Google Meet and Google Translate
- Phishing and identifying attacks
- Dangers of social media and staying safe online
- Understanding your digital footprint

An unusually grounded experience — explaining fundamental digital safety to people who interact with technology daily but have had little formal guidance on it.

## Volunteer — Turkey & Syria Earthquake Relief
**Emirates Red Crescent (ERC) · Bridges of Good Initiative**

Participated in packing food and aid boxes for families affected by the Turkey and Syria earthquakes, as part of a joint effort between Global Initiatives and the Red Crescent. Each box was packed with essential goods; handwritten cards from child volunteers were included to offer support and reassurance to recipients.

## Volunteer — Beach Clean-Up Drive
**AIESEC · Sufoh Beach, Jumeirah, Dubai**

Took part in a plogging drive jointly organised by AIESEC, Rotaract Cosmopolitan, and Halliday Growth at Sufoh Beach. Collected waste ranging from cigarette butts to plastic, using the Zeloop app to log and earn rewards for contributions.
